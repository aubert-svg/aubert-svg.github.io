About Me

I completed a Master's degree in Pure Mathematics, followed by a Structured Master at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS), South Africa. I later participated in the Pre-PhD program at the International Centre for Theoretical Physics (ICTP), Italy. I am passionate about mathematics, particularly combinatorics and geometry.
